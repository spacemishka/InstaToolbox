1. Image Editing Tools

1.1 Swipeable Photo Generator

Requirement:The system shall allow the user to select an image and automatically divide it into multiple square segments (e.g. 3, 6, or 9 parts) for use in swipeable Instagram carousel posts.

1.2 No-Crop Image Posting

Requirement:The system shall enable users to resize images to fit Instagram’s square or story formats without cropping, by adding padding or background blur.

1.3 9-Grid Feed Creator

Requirement:The system shall allow the user to split a single image into a 3×3 (or configurable) grid layout for uploading to their Instagram feed to form a larger composite image.

2. Video Processing Tools

2.1 Video Splitter

Requirement:The system shall accept a video input and automatically divide it into consecutive 15-second segments suitable for Instagram Stories.

3. Content Management & Reposting

3.1 Repost Tool (Without Watermark)

Requirement:The system shall allow users to repost Instagram images and videos without adding watermarks, while optionally including credit to the original author.

4. Creative Enhancements

4.1 Glitch & Vintage Effects

Requirement:The system shall provide a collection of at least 10 creative visual effects (e.g. glitch, retro, vintage) that can be applied to user images before posting.

4.2 Color Picker Tool

Requirement:The system shall allow users to select an image and extract prominent colors with their respective names (e.g. HTML or Pantone names) for caption inspiration or color matching.

4.3 Custom Fonts Generator

Requirement:The system shall offer various text styles and transformations (e.g. bold, italic, 𝐬𝑒𝑛𝑒𝑜𝑚𝑠, 𝕏𝔢𝔯𝔯𝔞, upside-down) for use in Instagram bios and captions.

5. Text Formatting & Metadata

5.1 Space Adder (Clean Caption Tool)

Requirement:The system shall remove unwanted characters (dots, dashes, line breaks) from user captions and format them cleanly for Instagram.

5.2 Hashtag Generator

Requirement:The system shall analyze user-provided content (e.g. a keyword or image) and suggest a list of trending or relevant hashtags for use in posts.

6. Content Aggregation

6.1 Top 9 Posts Collage

Requirement:The system shall allow users to select their most liked or engaged posts from the past year and automatically generate a 3×3 collage.

7. Link-in-Bio Page Builder

7.1 Taphere.bio Integration

Requirement:The system shall provide a feature to create and manage a mini landing page (link-in-bio) with customizable links, images, and descriptions.

8. Optional / Future Requirements

8.1 Feed Planner (Optional)

Requirement:The system may include a visual Instagram feed planner where users can preview and rearrange upcoming posts in a grid layout before publishing.

8.2 Gbox Keyboard (Android Only) (optional)

Requirement:The system shall provide a custom keyboard interface on Android that allows users to paste previously copied images and text directly into Instagram Stories or captions.
